Cold Coffee

Cold coffee is a refreshing, creamy, and slightly frothy beverage made by blending coffee with milk, sugar, and ice. It's a perfect drink for coffee lovers who enjoy a chilled and mildly sweet caffeine boost. Unlike traditional hot coffee, cold coffee is smooth, rich, and often topped with ice cream or chocolate syrup for extra indulgence.

It's popular in cafes and homes, especially during summer, as it provides the perfect balance of energy and refreshment. Some variations include Mocha Cold Coffee (with chocolate), Caramel Cold Coffee, and Frappe (blended with ice for a thicker texture).

Ingridients for Cold Coffee :

  • 1 cup chilled milk
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee powder (or brewed coffee)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust as per taste)
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream (optional, for a rich flavor)
  • Â½ cup cold water (if you like it lighter)

Steps for making cold coffee :

  1. In a blender, add coffee powder, sugar, and cold water. Blend for a few seconds until it gets frothy.
  2. Add chilled milk, ice cubes, and cream (if using). Blend again until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and top with vanilla ice cream for an extra treat.
  4. Garnish with chocolate syrup, cocoa powder, or grated chocolate if you like.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!
