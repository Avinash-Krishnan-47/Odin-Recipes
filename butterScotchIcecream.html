Butterscotch icecream

Butterscotch ice cream is a rich, creamy, and caramel-flavored dessert made with a blend of butter, brown sugar, and vanilla essence. It has a smooth texture with crunchy butterscotch praline bits, adding a delightful contrast. The ice cream gets its signature sweet, buttery taste from caramelized sugar and a hint of salt, making it a favorite among dessert lovers. Often drizzled with butterscotch sauce or topped with nuts, it’s a perfect treat for those who enjoy a mix of sweetness and crunch in every bite!

Ingredients for making Butterscotch :

  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts (cashews/almonds, optional)

Ingredients for making icecream :

  • 2 cups heavy cream (or fresh cream)
  • 1 cup full-fat milk
  • ½ cup condensed milk (for creaminess)
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp butterscotch essence
  • 2 tbsp butterscotch sauce (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions for making a butterscotch icecream :

  1. Make Butterscotch Praline (Crunchy Bits)
    1. Heat a pan on low flame, add ¼ cup sugar, and let it melt.
    2. Once the sugar caramelizes and turns golden brown , add butter and mix well
    3. Add chopped nuts (if using) and stir quickly
    4. Pour the caramel mixture onto a greased plate or parchment paper and let it cool.
    5. Once hardened, crush it into small pieces using a rolling pin.
  2. Prepare Ice Cream Base
    1. In a mixing bowl, whisk heavy cream until it becomes light and fluffy.
    2. Add milk, condensed milk, and sugar, then mix well.
    3. Stir in butterscotch essence and butterscotch sauce (if using).
    4. Fold in the crushed praline for a crunchy texture.
  3. Freeze & Serve
    1. Pour the mixture into an airtight container and freeze for at least 6-8 hours or overnight.
    2. Once set, scoop out the ice cream, garnish with extra praline and drizzle some butterscotch sauce.
    3. Serve and enjoy your homemade Butterscotch Ice Cream!
